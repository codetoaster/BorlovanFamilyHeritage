# Borlovan Family Heritage

Genealogy of the **Borlovan** and **Nai** families of Timișoara and the Banat, Romania —
the heritage of Monica (Borlovan) Black.

- **[The Borlovan Family Register](index.html)** (`borlovan_tree.html`) — interactive tree:
  fan chart, pedigree, sourced records.

Anchored by the obituaries of Mihai "Mike" Borlovan (1939–2021) and Doina (Nai) Borlovan
(1943–2013) of Timișoara, Romania and Mobile, Alabama — parents Iosif & Iuliana Borlovan
and Gavril & Iosana Nai. Research continuing in the Banat parish registers and Romanian
civil registration. Every fact is graded (confirmed / probable / speculative) and cited.
Living family members are redacted.

Maintained separately from the [Black Family Heritage](https://github.com/codetoaster/BlackFamilyHeritage) tree.
